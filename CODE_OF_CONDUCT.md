# Code of Conduct — Tableau Stimulator Enterprise

## Our Pledge

Tableau Stimulator Enterprise is an open-source project built to democratise data analytics for practitioners who lack access to expensive commercial tools. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and professional community.

## Our Standards

**Acceptable behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Giving credit where credit is due

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Dismissing contributions based on background or experience
